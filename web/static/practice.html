Practice Modes

Practice means that you play a bot rather than a real person. Choose from the options below for the bot's strategy.


Simple - Aggressive

Simple Aggressive mode means that the CPU will just set an attack objective for your spawner.


Simple - Defensive

Simple Defensive mode means that the CPU will just set an attack objective for your spawner, as well as create a single defensive wall and a subspawner near their spawner.
